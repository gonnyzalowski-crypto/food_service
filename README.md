# Gordon Food Service - Galveston

## Overview
Gordon Food Service - Galveston is a comprehensive food supply and provisioning platform serving the Gulf Coast region. Our platform enables businesses to manage their food supply chain efficiently, from ordering to delivery.

## Features

### Supply Management
- Browse and manage food inventory
- Real-time stock levels
- Category-based product organization

### Order Processing
- Streamlined ordering system
- Order history and tracking
- Reorder favorite items

### User Management
- Role-based access control
- Customer account management
- Secure authentication
